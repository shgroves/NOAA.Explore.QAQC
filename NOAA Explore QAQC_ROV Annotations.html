Introduction

The purpose of this document is to consolidate and review the ROV Annotations data collected during the ASPIRE campaign. In Step 1, the data are manually downloaded from SeaTube by entering each campaign number (EX1806, EX2206, etc.) Data are cleaned and reformatted using the clean_annotations.R script in the NOAA.Explore.QAQC R package.

The ASPIRE campaign was conducted from 2018 to 2022. The information presented here is from Okeanos Explorer ROV Annotations data collected in the following regions: SOUTH ATLANTIC BIGHT, CARIBBEAN, MID ATLANTIC and SOUTHEAST US, No Record, NORTHEAST U.S. & CANADA.
